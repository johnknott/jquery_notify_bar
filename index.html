jquery_notify_bar by ustrajunior

A simple gem to include jquery.notifyBar to your rails app.

This is a simple ruby gem to include jquery.nofityBar from Dmitri Smirnov to your rails app. jquery.notifyBar is a twitter like notification bar. With this gem, all your flash messages will be showed in the nofity bar.

Install

In your gem file add: gem 'jquery_notify_bar' You need copy this files to your stylesheets and javascripts folder http://ustrajunior.com/assets/jquery_notify_bar/stylesheets/jquery.notifyBar.css http://ustrajunior.com/assets/jquery_notify_bar/javascripts/jquery.notifyBar.js
